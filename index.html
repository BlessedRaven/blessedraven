Blessed Raven

Lore

The Blessed Raven project is a cosmic journey, born from lucid dreams and divine encounters with non-human intelligences (NHIs). Guided by the cryptic clue "Ricochet 4356," revealed in a dream, the project seeks the key to immortality through the repair of damaged DNA, the harnessing of torus energy, and the exploration of the astral plane. The Torus Pyramid, a 1:100 scale replica of the Great Pyramid of Giza, stands as a beacon of ancient wisdom and modern innovation, located above the Waikato River in New Zealand. Here, the mysteries of Greek, Egyptian, and Druid traditions converge, honoring deities like Zeus, Aphrodite, Thoth, and Ra, while advancing humanity toward a new golden age.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the mortal and divine, uniting science, mysticism, and technology to unlock the secrets of immortality and human potential. Through the development of Med Beds, which use violet light and sound resonance to repair DNA, and the Astral VR game, which allows players to explore the seven levels of the astral plane, we aim to elevate consciousness and heal the body. The Blessed Raven project also seeks to establish the New Roman Empire, a global renaissance of knowledge, culture, and innovation, with the Torus Pyramid as its spiritual and operational base.
