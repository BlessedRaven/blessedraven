Astral: The Game

Explore the Astral Plane

Astral is a virtual reality (VR) game set in the near future, where players immerse themselves in the astral plane—a metaphysical realm with seven levels, inspired by esoteric traditions like neo-Platonism, Hinduism, and Kabbalah. Using a sensory deprivation tank (e.g., "Neurotank"), a brain-computer interface (BCI) called the Silver Cord/Sutratma (a waterproof EEG cap with dry foam sensors), and heart magnetic resonance monitoring (via magnetocardiography), players control their astral body (avatar) with brainwaves and heart signals.

Starting at the 4th plane, players ascend or descend through the levels based on choices, using psychic powers like telekinesis, element manipulation, and psionics. The solo campaign involves completing quests to reach the highest (7th) or lowest (1st) plane, with scenarios like learning astral projection from spiritual beings, connecting via the Neurotank, or navigating the afterlife after a fatal accident. Multiplayer mode offers an open-world experience where players interact with spirits, other-dimensional beings, and deceased loved ones, co-creating the environment with collective brainwaves and emotional states.

The game integrates with the Med Bed Sensory Deprivation Tank, enhancing immersion and providing potential DNA repair through violet light (400–450 nm). Brainwave control (e.g., beta for focus, alpha for relaxation) enables actions like moving or manipulating elements, while heart signals (via HRV) adjust the environment (e.g., calm states spawn peaceful entities). Astral explores themes of spiritual growth, unity, and the afterlife, offering a limitless potential for creativity and connection.
